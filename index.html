The Social Justice Classroom's Mission

The Social Justice Classroom hosts participating instructor's syllabi, assignment sheets, rubrics, lesson plans, and many more genres that other instructors can employ in their classrooms to promote anti-racist pedagogy and social justice issues. Please scroll down to peruse genres you may want to implement in your classroom. Each genre is then organized by discipline and when available, is paired with an interview from the contributor to contextualize the material.
